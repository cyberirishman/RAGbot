This 3D visualization demonstrates how words can be represented in a semantic vector space. Each word is positioned according to its relative values on three dimensions:

Gender: Where the word falls on a spectrum from masculine to feminine.

Powerfulness: How much power or authority the word connotes.

Humanness: Whether the word refers to humans or non-human entities.

The spatial relationships between words reveal semantic similarities and differences, showing how meaning can be encoded geometrically.
